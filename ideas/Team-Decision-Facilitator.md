# Team Decision Facilitator

## üí° Concept
AI that analyzes team decisions, predicts outcomes of different choices, and highlights trade-offs to guide group decision-making. Think "AI strategic advisor for managers."

## üéØ How It Works
1. Team inputs decision context: problem, options, constraints, goals
2. AI analyzes historical data, similar decisions, team dynamics
3. Generates outcome predictions for each option (risk, cost, time, impact)
4. Highlights trade-offs and blind spots the team might miss
5. Facilitates discussion with structured frameworks (SWOT, cost-benefit)
6. Tracks decision outcomes over time to improve predictions
